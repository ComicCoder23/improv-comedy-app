# Data Permissions Notes

## Identified Risks

| Risk | Description | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram scraping | Meta TOS prohibits automated scraping; account/IP bans possible | High |
| Copyright — posters and images | Event posters are often copyrighted by designers; copying without permission is infringement | High |
| Personal data | GDPR applies — storing performer names, emails, locations requires lawful basis | High |
| Outdated event information | Old events left live can mislead users and damage trust | Medium |
| Incorrect ticket information | Linking to wrong or expired ticket pages creates user frustration | Medium |
| Community moderation | User-submitted content may be inaccurate, offensive, or spam | Medium |
| Defamation / reputation | False or misleading performer/team information could create liability | Medium |
| Consent and profile-claiming | Publishing a performer's profile without their knowledge or consent | Medium |

---

## Safer Operating Rules

### Data Collection
- Use public links — do not copy or mirror third-party content
- Get consent where possible before creating profiles for real people
- Let users submit and edit their own events, profiles, and listings
- Use admin review before any submission goes live

### Storage and Privacy
- Do not store personal contact details in public-facing records
- Keep an audit trail of source links for every imported or curated listing
- Provide a clear removal and opt-out process on the site
- Do not copy bios, images, or descriptions from other platforms without permission

### Automation Rules
- Do not automate DMs or outreach to performers, organisers, or venues
- Do not run bulk scraping scripts against Instagram, Facebook, or Eventbrite
- If using public APIs (Eventbrite, Google), stay within their rate limits and TOS

### GDPR Notes (UK)
- Lawful basis for processing personal data: consent or legitimate interest
- Users must be able to request data deletion
- Privacy policy required before launch
- Do not share personal data with third parties without consent
