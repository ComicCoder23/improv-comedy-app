# Comedy Admin Pain Points

## Identified Pain Points

- Scattered event information across Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, Eventbrite, and venue websites
- Too many WhatsApp and Facebook threads — easy to miss announcements
- Posters disappear quickly from feeds and are not searchable
- Beginners do not know where to start or who to contact
- No single shared calendar for the local comedy scene
- Teams and troupes lack public profiles that non-members can find
- Teachers need better workshop visibility beyond their personal networks
- Venues and studios are hard to compare or discover
- Collaboration depends on existing social circles — closed ecosystem
- Festival deadlines are easy to miss without a central tracker
- Booking and ticket links are fragmented across platforms
- Manual promotion takes too much time for small teams
