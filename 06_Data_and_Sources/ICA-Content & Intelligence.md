## Part 4: The Glasgow Improv Slang Dictionary
*Inside jokes to be used as 'Easter Eggs' throughout the app interface.*

* **Gallus:** Used for a bold, high-stakes move or a 'big choice.'
* **Haud yer Wheesht:** The polite (or not) way to tell a monologuing improviser to let someone else speak.
* **Yer bum’s out the windae:** When someone is clearly denying the reality of the scene or talking nonsense.
* **Gie it Laldy:** When the scene/song is going perfectly and you need to escalate to 100%.
* **Pish the bed:** A major technical fail (like missing a 'button' or a terrible callback).
