**Haud yer wheesht and listen up.**

This isn't just an app. This is a lighthouse for the **Pirates** who crash through a first beat with nothing but vibes and a dream. It’s a calibration tool for the **Robots** who are still trying to find the mathematical logic in a scene about a sentient deep-fried Mars Bar. And for the occasional **Ninja** who glides through a Harold making us all look like we’ve had a nightmare—we see you, and we’re coming for your secrets.

We’ve seen the **Comedy Geeks** in the front row armed with 11-letter words, waiting to watch you drown. We’ve seen the **Diamonds in the Rough** walk on stage for the first time, "nae scooby" what a Pattern Game is, and absolutely destroy the room.

### For the Neuro-Gallus
Most of all, this is for the **Neuro-Divergent** legends. For the ADHD brains with 47 tabs open, where "hyper-focus" is a superpower and every random thought is a potential third-beat callback. Improv is our playground. It’s where our busy minds finally make sense. 

It’s healing. It builds the kind of **Gallus** confidence that carries you through the "real world" when the lights go down. It’ll fix your social battery and quiet the "noise" in your nut—but let’s be clear: **It will not heal a broken leg.** If you fall off that SBT stage, call a medic; if you head-butt the beam at **OHD**, get some ice. Comedy is magic, but it’s no’ a miracle cure for stupidity.

### The Mission
The **Stage Manager** is watching. We’re collecting the lore, the "Glesga Patter," and the "Not Again" moments to build a tool that finally speaks our language. 

* **The Goal:** To move from "Mangled" sets to "Pure Dead Brilliant" Harolds.

* **The Choice:** We are here by choice, and that is our superpower.
