## Interview Questions — By User Type

### Organisers / Festival Runners
- How do you currently promote your events?
- What platforms do you use and what frustrates you most about them?
- Would you submit events directly to a directory?
- What information would you want displayed about your event?
- How do you handle ticket sales and booking?

### Teachers / Workshop Leaders
- How do beginners find your classes right now?
- What is the biggest barrier to filling your workshops?
- Would you want a public profile page for your classes?
- How do you handle bookings and enquiries?

### Experienced Performers
- How do you find jams, festivals, and collaboration opportunities?
- Do you have a public profile people can find?
- What information do you wish was easier to find in the scene?

### Beginners
- How did you first hear about improv/comedy?
- What stopped you from getting involved sooner?
- What would have made it easier to take that first step?
- What would you want to see on a beginners page?

### Venues / Studios
- Do you currently list your space for comedy/improv hire?
- How do comedy groups find you right now?
- Would you want a venue profile on a comedy directory?
- What information would venues need displayed?
